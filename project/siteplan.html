SkillBridge

Connect Your Skills to Opportunities

Site Name

SkillBridge

I chose this name because the website’s goal is to help beginners in tech connect their skills to job opportunities and organize their progress. The name expresses the idea of bridging skills with career growth.

Site Purpose

SkillBridge is a job and portfolio tracking website designed for new developers and tech learners. The site will help users:

  • Track their job applications and progress
  • Store and display personal projects in a portfolio section
  • Stay organized during job search activities
  • Access basic tips and resources for improving job applications

Scenarios

  • How can I keep track of the jobs I have applied for?
  • Where can I view all my personal projects in one place?
  • How do I update the status of a job application?

Color Schema

The following are the main colors I will use:

  • Deep Navy (#0a1a3d) – Used for headers, navigation bar, and important accents.
  • Sunset Orange (#ffb86b) – Used for call-to-action buttons and highlights.
  • Soft Blue Tint (#e4f0ff) – Used for page backgrounds and light sections.

Typography

I selected the following fonts:

  • Montserrat – For headings to create a modern and bold look.
  • Roboto – For paragraph text because it's simple and readable.
